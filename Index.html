Meri Laaj Mere Khwaja

1. Meri Laaj mere khwaja yuhi umr bhar nibhana.

Kahi is ghulam ko tum khwaja na bhul jaana.

2. Ye Nahi mujhe gawara kahi aur jaake maangu.

Rakhe khuda salamat khaja ka aastana.

3. Mujhe raas agayi hai khaja teri ghulami.

Mujhe jaanta hai khaja tere naam se zamana.

4. Jabtak hai jaan baakhi jabtak ye zindagi hai.

Khaja ke aastan par rahe yuhi aana jaana.

5. Mujhe iska gham nahi hai ke badal gaya zamana.

Meri zindagi ke malik kahi tum badal na jaana.

6. Hoon surquru jahan me ye sab tera karam hai.

Mujhe duniya jaanti hai khaja ka hun diwana.
